About the Grid

The Grid is an arrangement of anime that I've enjoyed over the years. Most of these I rated at least an 8 or higher on myanimelist at the time I watched them. There's some shows on here I don't reccomend in retrospect. There's also some shows that, while I enjoyed them and still think they are good, I probably wouldn't reccomend them to others. It was a big grid to fill, so I was a little forgiving in my inclusion of some (I even cheated a bit and included a visual novel in there!).

I haven't ordered these in any particular fashion. Mostly I just wanted to get the art to fit well in given shapes. Some of my favourites did end up getting larger shapes, but that was more a consequence of me thinking of them first when constructing the grid
